Getting the best results

How to shoot photos we can work magic with

You don't need a studio — just a clear photo. A few simple habits make your visuals sharper, more accurate and faster to produce.

Try a free test sample Start an order

Do

  • Keep it clear & sharp — in focus, no motion blur.
  • Use a neutral, uncluttered background.
  • Light it well and evenly — daylight works great.
  • Show the full product, plus a few close-ups of key details.
  • Send the highest resolution you have.

Avoid

  • Blurry, dark or low-light shots.
  • Busy or distracting backgrounds.
  • Heavy filters or strong colour casts.
  • Tiny, low-res screenshots.
  • Cropping part of the product out of frame.
