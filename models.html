How a custom model comes together

A short, collaborative process — then your model is locked in for every future order.

01

Intake

You share references and a brief on the look, age and vibe you're after.

02

2–3 directions

We design a few distinct model directions for you to react to.

03

You choose

Pick the face that fits your brand best — with a round of refinement.

04

Locked in

We lock the final model so it stays perfectly consistent going forward.

05

Used in every order

From then on, your model appears in every visual you order.

What you provide

  • A few references for the look you have in mind.
  • The age, vibe and look that fits your brand.
  • Any clothing or styling direction.
  • Your do's and don'ts — anything to include or avoid.

Pricing & timing

A custom model is a one-time design fee, then it's yours to reuse across every future order at the normal visual price. Because we design the model up front, the first order has a longer lead time — after that, every order runs at the usual speed.
