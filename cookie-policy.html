What cookies are

Cookies are small text files a website can store on your device. They're commonly used to make sites work, remember preferences, and measure how a site is used.

How we use them

The VISUAILS website is largely static. By default we only use essential cookies needed for the site to function and to remember your privacy choices. We do not load advertising or tracking cookies without your consent.

  • Essential — basic functionality and security. Always on.
  • Analytics — anonymous usage measurement. Only with your consent.

Managing cookies

You can accept or decline non-essential cookies when asked, and clear or block cookies at any time in your browser settings. Blocking essential cookies may affect how the site works.
