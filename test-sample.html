Give us a good photo, get a great result

The clearer your input, the better the visual we can build from it.

Do

  • Clear, sharp and in focus
  • Neutral, uncluttered background
  • Good, even lighting
  • The whole product in frame

Don't

  • Blurry or low-resolution shots
  • Dark or heavily shadowed photos
  • Busy or distracting backgrounds
  • Heavy filters or watermarks
